### Key Recommendations for Your Site
- **Start with Low-Traffic Friendly Models**: Affiliates and donations (e.g., expand your Buy Me a Coffee link) suit zero/low traffic. Promote poker trackers/apps (e.g., via Amazon Affiliates or direct programs from Poker Bankroll Tracker) in blog posts—poker users seek these tools.
- **Scale with Traffic Growth**: As you advertise and gain users, add ads (AdSense for starters) and sponsored content (e.g., from poker sites). Aim for medium traffic via SEO (e.g., "poker bankroll tips") or X/Twitter promotion.
- **Poker-Specific Tips**: Integrate affiliates for related products (e.g., books, software like PokerTracker). Avoid heavy ads to maintain trust in financial tracking. Track your own site's performance to refine (e.g., 1–5% conversion boosts income 2x).
- **Potential Overall**: With diversification, low traffic could yield $50–$500/month; medium $1,000–$5,000; high $10,000+. Test 2–3 models (e.g., affiliates + donations) and use analytics to optimize—many poker sites earn $1K+/month at medium traffic via affiliates alone.
