This guide provides a quick reference for typical HUD (Heads-Up Display) statistics like VPIP, PFR, and 3-Bet % for common online poker player archetypes. Use these ranges to help classify opponents you encounter at the tables.

Player Archetype Typical VPIP % Typical PFR % Typical 3-Bet % Description
NIT (Rock) 8-15 6-12 1-4 Extremely tight, plays only premium hands. Very passive post-flop unless they have the nuts.
TAG (Tight-Aggressive) 18-25 15-22 5-9 A strong, balanced player. Plays a selective range of hands aggressively. The typical "regular".
LAG (Loose-Aggressive) 26-35 20-30 8-12 Plays many hands and applies constant pressure through betting and raising. Can be tricky to play against.
Fish (Calling Station) 40-70 5-20 1-5 Plays a very wide range of hands passively. Calls down often with weak holdings. Rarely bluffs.
