The Scenario

Follow this example to see how StakeEasy.net tracks your poker finances.

1
First Deposit

You join a poker site and deposit $50. Your total bankroll is $50 and your profit is $0.

2
Winning Sessions

After playing, your site balance grows to $200. Your bankroll is $200 and your profit is $150.

3
Invest in an Asset

You move $50 into Bitcoin. Now: sites = $150, assets = $50, profit = $150, deposits = $50.

4
Keep Grinding

You play more and run your site balance up to $350. You are now profiting $350 in total.

5
Make a Withdrawal

You withdraw $100 for groceries. Your site balance drops to $250.

6
Final Position

Sites: $250 + Assets: $50 = Bankroll: $300. Your profit is $350.

{# ── Section 2: The Calculation ── #}

The Calculation

StakeEasy.net uses two simple formulas to track your finances.

Bankroll Formula

Total Bankroll = Poker Sites + Assets

$300 = $250 + $50

Profit Formula

Profit = Poker Sites + Assets − Deposits + Withdrawals

$350 = $250 + $50 − $50 + $100

{# ── Section 3: How To Use ── #}

How to Use StakeEasy.net

A step-by-step guide to managing your poker bankroll.

{# Timeline-style steps #}
Record Your Starting Bankroll

Set aside a sum you can afford to lose. Go to the Deposits page and add a deposit for that amount.

Add Your Poker Sites

Go to the Poker Sites page and add each site you play on — set its name, currency, and the amount you've bought in for.

Update Regularly

After every session, or at regular intervals, update the balance on each poker site. StakeEasy.net keeps a history of every change.

Record Withdrawals

When you take money out for personal spending, add a withdrawal. Your profit stays accurate because the formula accounts for it.

Track Your Assets

Set aside part of your bankroll into investments — cash, crypto, gold, silver, stocks. Add them on the Assets page and they'll count towards your total bankroll.

Multi-Currency — Handled Automatically

Play in GBP, EUR, or any currency — StakeEasy.net converts everything to USD using live exchange rates so your totals are always accurate.

Good Luck at the Tables!

That's it — if you go bust, just deposit again and keep going. StakeEasy.net will track it all. From the team, we wish you the best of luck!
