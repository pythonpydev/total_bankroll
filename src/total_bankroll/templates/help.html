How to get the most out of StakeEasy.net

The scenario for you as a poker player:

  • When you first join a poker site, you will initially deposit cash, for example, $50.
  • At this point your total bankroll is $50 and your profit is $0.
  • After playing for a while, your latest poker site balance is $200.
  • Now, your total bankroll is $200 and your net profit is now $150.
  • At this point you decide to move $50 from the poker site and invest it in Bitcoin.
  • Now, your cash on poker sites is $150, your assets are $50, your net profit is still $150, and your total deposits are $50.
  • You play a while longer, and now have $350 on the poker site (you are now profiting $350).
  • You then decide to withdraw $100 to buy some groceries.
  • At this point you have $250 on the poker site, $50 in assets (Bitcoin), after making an initial deposit of $50 and withdrawing $100. Your total bankroll is $300, and your profit is $350.

How total bankroll and profit is calculated

  • Using the previous scenario, the formula used on StakeEasy.net to calculate your bankroll is as follows:
  • Total Bankroll = Amount on Poker Sites + Assets
  • Profit = Amount on Poker Sites + Assets - Deposits + Withdrawals
  • Total Bankroll = $250 + $50 = $300
  • Profit = $250 + $50 - $50 + $100 = $350
